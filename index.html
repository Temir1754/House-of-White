The Studio

A studio built on restraint.

House of White was founded on a simple belief — that the best interiors are quiet ones. We design with proportion, natural light and materials that age beautifully, so a home feels considered rather than decorated.

Every project is led personally, from the first sketch to the final piece of furniture. No outsourcing of taste, no guesswork — one point of view, carried all the way through.

— Samal Iskakova
